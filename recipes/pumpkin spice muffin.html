Pumpakin Spice Muffin

Description

Do you miss the fall? This recipe brings back an authentic autumn feeling with pumpkin spice. Try it out!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 250g pumpkin pure
  • 200g flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 1 tbsp gingerbread mix
  • 30g butter
  • 80g oil
  • 1 baking powder

Steps

  1. Bake the pumpkin then slice it into cubes.
  2. Add in the oil and melted butter then blend until smooth.
  3. Mix in the eggs and the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Bake at 180 degrees until 20-25 mins.
