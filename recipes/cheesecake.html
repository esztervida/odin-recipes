Cheesecake

Description

Delicious cheesecake baked in the oven, served with fresh fruits.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 200g sour cream
  • 200g sugar
  • 400g cream cheese
  • 2 dl heavy cream
  • 2 tbps strach
  • 1 tsp vanilla aroma
  • 300g biscuits
  • 120g butter

Steps

  1. Smash the biscuits then mix with the melted butter.
  2. Press down in a pan. bake at 180 degrees for 12 mins.
  3. Mix the other ingredients, then add in the eggs one by one at the end.
  4. Wrap the pan with aluminium foil then add water. Bake at 150 degrees for 90 mins.
