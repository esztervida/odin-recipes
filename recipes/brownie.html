Brownie

Description

It calls for basic pantry ingredients, and it’s hardly more difficult than using a mix. As for the brownies, they’re true perfection – glossy, fudgy, and filled with rich chocolate flavor. Trust me, you’re going to want to make them ASAP!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 110g butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla aroma

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, butter and vanilla.
  3. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 30 minutes at 180 degrees.
